# Governance

Specter is maintained by the core maintainers listed in `MAINTAINERS.md`.

## Decision Making
- Day-to-day decisions are made by maintainers.
- Significant changes require a design discussion and review.
- Security-related changes follow `SECURITY.md`.

## Releases
- Releases are tagged and follow semantic versioning.
- Changes are documented in `CHANGELOG.md`.

## Code of Conduct
All community interactions follow `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`.
